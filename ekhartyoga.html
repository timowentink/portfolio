Slide in my mailbox

A brighter and better design for EkhartYoga, the video platform for doing yoga at home.

Thousands of students and teachers around the world have joined this online community and get unlimited acces to thousands of classes by top teachers and over 70 guided programs. They can read and save articles and recipes, make playlists and dive deeper to learn everything yoga in their library.

We helped EkhartYoga with their digital strategy while improving their platform with a new design, functionalities and an overall better user experience.

I was responsible for the entire user experience and visual design phase building this platform during my time at Frismedia. It was a great experience working with the cool people at EkhartYoga, learned a lot about yoga too.
