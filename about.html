About me

Freelance Digital Designer based in Utrecht, the Netherlands

Who I am
A Freelance Digital Designer based in Utrecht, the Netherlands who has likes to solve problems through design. I'm specialized in crafting user experiences, web and mobile apps and design systems.
What is my background
I have a bachelor's degree in Communication and Multimedia Design from Hogeschool Rotterdam, after completing a non-academic design education. After working at some smaller and medium size design studios I am now bringing food to the table as a freelance designer working for all types of studios, companies and brands.
What I do
I'm a problem solver, specialized in UI & UX. Which means I'm always looking for the right balance between form and interaction. You work with me to define, design and improve the experiences people have with your brand.
